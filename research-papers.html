  • Primes In Arithmetic Progressions And Idea For Goldbach's Conjecture

    I have independently developed a novel algorithm, referred to as the 'Exemption Rule,' which efficiently sieves out composite numbers in arithmetic progressions, leaving only prime numbers. This rule has applications in number theory and has been utilized to solve problems related to prime numbers. I have applied this rule to address challenges in Goldbach's Conjecture and other arithmetic progression-based problems. My work showcases a strong aptitude for algorithmic thinking and problem-solving in the field of mathematics.
